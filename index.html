About The Bristol Art Museum

The Bristol Art Museum helps to promote understanding. The various collections available allows people worldwide to understand various other cultures, other ways of thinking and how they are connected to each other. The collection spreads across the beginning of human history over millions of years ago to the present.

The Museum is a very distinctive resource to the world due to the wide range of its collection. It gives the general public the avenue to review cultural identities, also explore a wide network of interconnected human cultures all over the world.
